ABOUT ME

My name is kelly Vella.

I am 24 years old.

Stated in Malta.

Being part of the Design world, I have grown to love the feeling of finding inspiration; whether its in people, cities or books.

My aspiration in life is to inspire other people with my passion in art and design.

I am very fond of minimal design as I believe that 'simplicity is the key'.

I use my life experiences to express myself especially with my illustrations.

I enjoy reading books that are knowledgeable and quite deep.

Apart from digital art, I love to draw line works and also do oil-painting; mostly portraits.

Part of my dream is to actually have my own exhibition.

My favourite colour is Black.

The city that will forever inspire me is London.

I enjoy listening to people's approach of things even if they are strangers.

In my free time I like to workout or listen to music.

My favourite artist is Kendrick Lamar.

If you think we can create something interesting together, feel free to contact me.
